# Greek Dinner Party — Reference Menu

From the 5th June 2026 dinner party (Masha & Alex, 3 guests total).

## Confirmed Dish List

| Dish | Key Points |
|------|-----------|
| Chicken gyros | Marinate ahead, cook in oven or on griddle |
| Homemade oven chips | 200°C, ~25 min, toss in olive oil + oregano |
| Homemade flatbread | Dough needs 30 min rest; cook on hot hob ~2 min each side |
| Greek salad | Tomato, cucumber, red onion, olives, feta — prep early, chill |
| Tzatziki | Greek yoghurt + grated cucumber + garlic + dill — make ahead, chill |

## Oven Strategy

Both oven chips and gyros can go in at 200°C — likely same shelf or staggered by 10 min. Flatbread is hob-only. Salad and tzatziki are no-cook, make-ahead.

## Typical Timeline (serving at 19:00)

- **16:00** — Make tzatziki (chill)
- **16:15** — Marinate chicken (if not done earlier)
- **16:30** — Make flatbread dough (rest 30 min)
- **17:00** — Prep Greek salad (chill)
- **17:30** — Prep potatoes for oven chips
- **18:00** — Oven chips IN (200°C, 25 min)
- **18:10** — Chicken gyros IN (200°C, 20 min)
- **18:25** — Cook flatbreads on hob (2 min each, ~8 min total)
- **18:30** — Oven chips OUT
- **18:30** — Chicken OUT, rest 5 min, slice
- **18:40** — Assemble and serve
