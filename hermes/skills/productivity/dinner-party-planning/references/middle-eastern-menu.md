# Middle Eastern Dinner Party Menu

This reference was created after planning a Middle Eastern–inspired dinner party for Jill, Adam, Sid & Grace on Saturday 20th June 2026.

## Menu

| Dish | Notes |
|------|-------|
| Chicken Shawarma | Main centrepiece. Marinate overnight. Grill or oven. |
| Homemade Pita Bread | Cook last so they arrive warm. Dough can be made the day before. |
| Hummus from Scratch | Make in the morning. Ice cube trick for creaminess. |
| Quick Pickled Red Onions | Make the day before. Ready in 10 min + 30 min cooling. |
| Quinoa Salad with Roasted Vegetables | Room temp side. Make ahead. Dairy-free (no feta). |

## Dietary Notes

- All dishes are **dairy-free** and **kosher-friendly**
- No tahini issues (it's sesame, not dairy)
- Quinoa salad omits feta for dairy-free version
